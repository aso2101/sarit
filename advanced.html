Brief Search Term Help

  • Enter search terms with utf-8 characters: e.g., ā ī ū ṛ ḷ ṅ ñ ṭ ḍ ṇ ś ṣ ṁ ḥ

  • Match patterns with wildcard characters: e.g., rūp.*to retrieve rūpam, rūpeṇāpi&c..

  • Do not use punctuation marks: e.g., search for apāya heturather than apāya-hetu.

  • Similar word search searches the whole database.
